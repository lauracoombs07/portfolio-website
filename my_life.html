My Life

Family

The most important thing in my life, is my family. I have an incredible, supportive husband, Ben, and five amazing kids. Cameron, Sawyer, Kylie, Grayson, and Alexia. They are my world. I'll be completely honest, I am not an animal lover, but my children are. That makes our pets an important part of our family. We have two dogs, Oki and Korra and a Turtle named Bubbles.

Ben

Ben and I have been married for 16 and a half years. We met working at a Youth Treatment Center in 2005. We had decided when we had our first child, Cameron, that one of us would always be home with the kids.

Five years ago, I told Ben that I wanted to go back to work. He was completely supportive. We made the adjustments required, and I started working full time. Since that day, Ben has done everything in his power to make sure that I can be successful in my career. He is my biggest cheerleader and my best friend. I am so grateful for him.
