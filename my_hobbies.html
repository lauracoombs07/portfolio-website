Baking

Back to Top

I have always loved baking. I have been baking since I was a teenager. Many people believe that baking and cooking are the same thing, but they are not! I cannot cook to save my life. Baking, however, comes far more naturally to me. I love the precision of baking. I love that I can follow a recipe and create something delicious. I love that I can share my creations with my family and friends. I love that I can create something that brings joy to others. My 3 favorite things to bake are Pies, Breads and Cinnamon Rolls.
